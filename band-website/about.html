Shane Filan

Born: 5 July, 1979

Genres: Pop

Shane Steven Sherlock Filan is an Irish singer and songwriter. He was one of the two lead singers and frontman of boy band Westlife during the group's duration from 1998 to 2012 and from 2018 up to the present. Filan has since released three solo albums: You and Me, Right Here and Love Always.

Markus Feehily

Born: May 28, 1980

Genres: Pop, Soul, R&B

Markus Michael Patrick Verdon Feehily is an Irish singer and songwriter. He was one of the lead singers of the boy band Westlife from 1998 to 2012. He is the highest charting LGBT performer with most number one appearances in the UK Singles Chart.

Kian Egan

Born: April 29, 1980

Genres: Pop

Kian John Francis Kelly Egan is an Irish singer and songwriter, best known as a former member of boy band Westlife. He is a coach on The Voice of Ireland and won the thirteenth series of I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! in 2013.

Nicky Byrne

Born: October 9, 1978

Genres: Pop

Nicholas Bernard James Adam McGarry Byrne, Jr. is an Irish singer, songwriter, radio presenter, dancer, television presenter and former professional footballer, best known for being a member of Irish music band Westlife; he was the band's oldest member.
